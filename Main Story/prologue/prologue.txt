# PROLOGUE — "An Ordinary Day (Until It Wasn't)"

---

## SCENE 1 — HOMEROOM

*It is an unremarkable Tuesday afternoon and as usual you would occasionally hear explosion on the science lab. The kind where the sky is too bright to look at and the classroom smells like chalk dust and someone's leftover lunch. Half the seats are empty before the final period even starts.*

---

> **To Shuu:** You're at the back of the classroom, feet up, jacket draped over the chair like it owes you something. What are you doing right now?

> **To Biron:** You're somewhere in the middle of the room, face down on a textbook that has not been opened today, yesterday, or arguably this semester. You're awake — barely. What does waking up here look like for you?

> **To Mari:** You're near the front. You've probably already noticed Biron is asleep and made a mental note about it. What are you actually doing right now?

*Let them establish themselves. One or two exchanges. Don't rush it.*

---

*Then — movement outside the classroom door. Fast footsteps. A girl in a lab coat shoots past the window in the door, clutching something to her chest, hair coming loose, grinning like she just did something she absolutely should not have done.*

*Three seconds behind her: the student council.*

*The door bursts open. Yumemi Okazaki — student council president, two years running, the kind of person who color-codes her contingency plans — stops in the doorway, scans the room, doesn't find what she's looking for.*

> "Did anyone see Kitashirakawa come through here?"

*She doesn't wait for an answer. Already moving. One of the council members shouts back over their shoulder:*

> "She destroyed the physics lab equipment again — Kamimura-sensei is furious, the council is filing a destruction of property report—"

*Then they're gone. The hallway goes quiet.*

---

> **DM:** Chiyuri Kitashirakawa — third year, science club, your mutual disaster of a friend — just ran past this classroom being chased by the entire student council. You have about thirty seconds before this gets further away.

> Shuu — that looked like a fight worth watching. Biron — you caught maybe forty percent of that. Something about Chiyuri. Mari — you caught all of it, and Chiyuri looked like she was having the time of her life, which is usually a bad sign.

> Do you go after them, or do you stay put?

---

## SCENE 2 — THE ANNOUNCEMENT

### BRANCH A — They stayed put or lost the chase

*A minute passes. The hallway stays quiet.*

*Then the PA crackles.*

> *"Attention! This is Chiyuri Kitashirakawa from the Science Research Club! My experiment has just produced a completely successful result—"*

*Scuffling. Someone grabbing for the microphone.*

> *"This announcement is unauthorized—"* — Yumemi —

> *"—the destruction was incidental to the success—"*

*The PA cuts dead.*

---

> **DM:** That's all you got. Chiyuri's experiment worked. The one nobody would tell you anything about. Do you move?

---

### BRANCH B — They gave chase

> **DM:** Give me an **Athletics or Acrobatics check, DC 13.**

*On a failure — falls into Branch A.*

*On a success:*

> You round the corner just as Chiyuri spots you — and that's her mistake. She's so distracted that she doesn't see Yumemi cut off the left. Two council members close from the right. She's pinned against the wall in seconds.

> **Yumemi:** "You're filing a full report for every piece of equipment you destroyed. Do you understand me?"

> *"Let me go! I have successfully — look it doesn't matter, the point is it worked and I need to check on it right now—"*

*The floor moves.*

> *"...uhhh. maybe a slight miscalculation somewhere. ahehe."*

> **→ Scene 3 begins.**

---

## SCENE 3 — THE QUAKE

*It starts small. The floor shifts — longer this time. A desk scrapes on its own. Something falls off a shelf down the hall. The lights flicker.*

**Branch A:**
*The door swings open. Chiyuri, breathless, grabs the frame.*

> *"Okay so — we need to move. Now. Somewhere open, away from the walls—"*

> **DM:** She looks genuinely rattled for the first time today. What do you do?

**Branch B:**
*Chiyuri stops laughing. She looks around the hallway.*

> *"Okay we need to move. Away from the walls — come on—"*

---

*The hum gets louder. The windows rattle. The normal sounds outside — voices, the sports club, traffic — just stop.*

> **DM:** It's not coming from one direction. It's coming from everywhere. What do you do?

*Let them act. Track where they are and what they grab.*

*Whatever they are carrying right now is what they bring with them.*

---

## SCENE 4 — THE RIFT

*It opens in the floor. Between you. A seam of light splits the linoleum and the hum becomes a tone you feel behind your teeth.*

*Chiyuri freezes. Whatever is in her jacket pocket starts glowing through the fabric. She doesn't get a chance to reach for it — the rift lurches and she gets pulled in, grabbing Yumemi on the way down. The rest of the student council follows.*

*Then it starts pulling at the three of you.*

> **DM:** Shuu, Biron, Mari — give me a **Strength or Dexterity check DC 15** to resist.

*On a success:*
> You hold your ground. You have a few seconds — grab what you can.
> **DC 10 check for every additional item you want to secure.**

*On a failure:*
> The rift takes you immediately.

*Either way — the rift takes all three of you.*

---

*The sensation is like being turned inside out. Colors with no names. You are moving very fast through something that is not space.*

> **DM:** Everyone roll a **d4.**

*Whatever they roll — they see one landscape through the fabric. Mountain. Village. Mansion. Bamboo. Just one.*

> **DM:** You can see somewhere through the fabric. Do you resist?

*On resist — **DC 13.** Success changes the vision, they roll d4 again and choose again. Failure — they land on what they saw.*

*On no resist — they land there.*

---

*Before the ground arrives — Mari. She's falling with you but something is wrong. The space is pulling at her differently. Like she's being pulled apart at the edges.*

*She doesn't look like she's in pain.*

*Then the ground arrives.*

> **→ LANDING**
