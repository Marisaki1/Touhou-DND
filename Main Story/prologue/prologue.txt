# PROLOGUE — "An Ordinary Day (Until It Wasn't)"

---

## SCENE 1 — AFTER SCHOOL

*It is an unremarkable Tuesday after school. The classroom is mostly empty. The hallway outside is winding down. Nothing is happening — aside from the distant explosion from the science lab, which stopped being notable about two months ago.*

---

> **To Shuu:** You've already flicked an eraser at Byron twice. The second one actually hit him. You're not sorry. You're bored out of your skull and Mari hasn't looked up in ten minutes which means she's not going to react either, and you need something to happen or you're going to start moving furniture. What are you doing right now?

> **To Byron:** Something hit you recently. You're pretty sure it was Shuu. You didn't move. You're not asleep but you're not exactly awake either — somewhere comfortable in between. What are you doing right now?

> **To Mari:** You noticed the eraser. Both of them. You didn't say anything. You've had years of practice deciding which battles are worth it. What are you actually doing right now?

*Let them establish themselves. One or two exchanges. Don't rush it.*

---

*Then — movement outside the classroom door. Fast footsteps. A girl in a lab coat shoots past the window in the door, clutching something to her chest, hair coming loose, grinning like she just did something she absolutely should not have done.*

*Three seconds behind her: the student council.*

*The door bursts open. Yumemi Okazaki — student council president, two years running, the kind of person who color-codes her contingency plans — stops in the doorway, scans the room, doesn't find what she's looking for.*

> **Yumemi:** "Did anyone see Ms. Kitashirakawa come through here?"

*She doesn't wait for an answer. Already moving. One of the council members shouts back over their shoulder:*

> "She destroyed the physics lab equipment again — Rikako-sensei is furious, the council is filing a destruction of property report —"

*Then they're gone. The hallway goes quiet.*

---

> **DM:** Chiyuri Kitashirakawa — third year, science club, your mutual disaster of a friend — just ran past this classroom being chased by the entire student council. You have about thirty seconds before this gets further away.

> Shuu — that looked like a fight worth watching. Byron — you caught maybe forty percent of that. Something about Chiyuri. Mari — you caught all of it, and Chiyuri looked like she was having the time of her life, which is usually a bad sign.

> Do you go after them, or do you stay put?

---

## SCENE 2 — THE ANNOUNCEMENT

### BRANCH A — They stayed put

*A minute passes. The hallway stays quiet.*

*Then the PA crackles.*

> **Chiyuri:** *"Attention! This is Chiyuri Kitashirakawa from the Science Research Club! My experiment has just produced a completely successful —"*

*Scuffling. The microphone changes hands.*

> **Yumemi:** *"This announcement is completely unauthorized —"*

> **Chiyuri:** *"— the destruction was incidental to the success, which is the important —"*

*The PA cuts dead.*

> **DM:** That's all you got. Do you move?

---

### BRANCH B — They gave chase

> **DM:** Give me an **Athletics or Acrobatics check, DC 13.**

**On a failure:**
> You lose her. The hallway splits three ways and she's already gone. You're standing in an empty corridor when the floor shifts.
> **→ Scene 3, Branch B Failure**

**On a success:**
> You round the corner just as Chiyuri spots you — and that's her mistake. She's so distracted she doesn't see Yumemi cut off the left. Two council members close from the right. Pinned.

> **Chiyuri:** *"UGYAA??!!"*

> **Yumemi:** "You are filing a full incident report for every single piece of equipment you destroyed. Do you understand me?"

> **Chiyuri:** *"Let me go! It worked, that's the part that matters, I need to check on it right —"*

*The floor moves.*

> **Chiyuri:** *She sticks her tongue out.* "...looks like there's a slight problem. Ahehe."

> **→ Scene 3, Branch B Success**

---

## SCENE 3 — THE QUAKE

**Branch A — They're in the classroom:**

*The floor shifts again. Longer this time. A desk scrapes. Something falls off a shelf down the hall.*

*The door swings open. Chiyuri grabs the frame — grinning, but her eyes are doing something her mouth isn't.*

> **Chiyuri:** *She sticks her tongue out.* "Okay so. Slight problem. We need to move — somewhere open, away from the walls, now —"

> **DM:** She looks genuinely rattled for the first time today. What do you do?

---

**Branch B Success — They're in the hallway with Chiyuri:**

*The floor shifts again. Everyone in the hallway grabs something — a wall, a door frame, each other.*

*Chiyuri goes still. Whatever she was about to argue evaporates.*

> **Chiyuri:** *Tongue out, grin gone wobbly.* "Slight problem. Away from the walls — all of you, now —"

---

**Branch B Failure — They're in the hallway, alone:**

*The floor shifts. Then again. The lights flicker.*

*Then Chiyuri rounds the corner at a dead sprint and nearly collides with them.*

> **Chiyuri:** *She doesn't stop moving.* "Oh good, you're here — slight problem — walk fast —"

---

*The hum gets louder. The windows rattle. The normal sounds outside — the baseball club, traffic, voices — just stop.*

> **DM:** It's not coming from one direction. It's coming from everywhere. What do you do?

*Let them act. Whatever they are carrying right now is what they bring with them.*

---

## SCENE 4 — THE RIFT

*It opens in the floor. Between you. A seam of light splits the linoleum and the hum becomes a tone you feel behind your teeth.*

*Chiyuri freezes. Whatever is in her jacket pocket starts glowing through the fabric. She doesn't get a chance to reach for it — the rift lurches and she gets pulled in, grabbing Yumemi on the way down. The rest of the student council follows.*

*Then it starts pulling at the three of you.*

> **DM:** Shuu, Byron, Mari — give me a **Strength or Dexterity check DC 15** to resist.

*On a success:*
> You hold your ground. You have a few seconds — grab what you can.
> **DC 10 check for every additional item you want to secure.**

*On a failure:*
> The rift takes you immediately.

*Either way — the rift takes all three of you.*

---

*The sensation is like being turned inside out. Colors with no names. You are moving very fast through something that is not space.*

> **DM:** Everyone roll a **d4.**

*Whatever they roll — they see one landscape through the fabric. Mountain. Village. Mansion. Bamboo. Just one.*

> **DM:** You can see somewhere through the fabric. Do you resist?

*On resist — **DC 13.** Success changes the vision, they roll d4 again and choose again. Failure — they land on what they saw.*

*On no resist — they land there.*

---

*Before the ground arrives — Mari. She's falling with you but something is wrong. The space is pulling at her differently. Like she's being pulled apart at the edges.*

*She doesn't look like she's in pain.*

*Then the ground arrives.*

> **→ LANDING**
